About Us

Rental Management System creates a suitable platform where tenants and landlords are able to interact based on a common purpose.

Landlords are able to upload their properties whereas tenants can create profiles and view available houses which they can rent at suitable prices.

Tenants to be are able to communicate with landlords via whatsApp, facebook, or pungwa platform for further verifications concerning the property advertised by the landlords.

Landlords must buy units in order to advertise their property to tenants .
